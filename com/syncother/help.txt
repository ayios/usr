SYNOPSIS
  sync distribution from another destination

 USAGE
  sync --from=dest [options]

 DESCRIPTION
  sync distribution from another destination

 OPTIONS

  --from=            directory tree (required)
	 --copyinteractive  confirm when copy (default yes)
  --verboseoff       turn verbose off  (default yes)
  --ign_verbose      turn verbose on, while ignoring on sync
  --ign_rm_verbose   turn verbose on, while ignoring on remove
